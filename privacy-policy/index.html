Collecting and Using Your Personal Data

Personal Data

While using our Service, you may be requested to provide certain personal information that can be used for identification. This may include, but is not limited to:

Usage Data

Usage Data is automatically collected when you interact with our Service. This may include details such as your device’s IP address, browser type, and pages visited, along with the duration of those visits.

We utilize cookies and similar tracking technologies to monitor activity within our Service and to store relevant information. These technologies may include:
