Dive into the Excitement with Every Match!

Build your team and win together with EliteUnited! Our platform takes your passion for football to new heights, offering everything you need to create your ultimate fantasy team. Whether you’re an experienced player or just starting, you can elevate your game and experience the excitement of competition like never before.

Discover how we elevate your game and help you strategize for victory, with tools and resources that ensure every decision counts!

Step 1: Sign Up & Download the App

  • Start your journey by clicking the “Download the App” button found on our homepage. This is your gateway to a thrilling fantasy football adventure!
  • Enter your mobile number in the field provided and tap “Send Me the Link”. This simple step connects you to our vibrant community.
  • Shortly after, expect a text message with a direct link to download the app. Keep an eye on your SMS for our welcome message!
  • Install the app, set up your personal profile by filling in your details, and get ready to make your mark in the fantasy arena!

Step 2: Select a Match

  • Browse our carefully selected list of upcoming football matches across various leagues. Your next thrilling contest is just a few clicks away!
  • Choose a match and select a contest that fits your interests and level of expertise. From casual play to intense competition, there’s something for everyone!

Step 3: Build Your Dream Team

  • Engage in the exciting process of creating your unique 11-player squad while staying within your budget. Every choice impacts your success!
  • Don’t forget to pick your captain and vice-captain wisely, as they earn enhanced points—2X for the captain and 1.5X for the vice-captain. This vital choice can change the game!

Step 4: Join Contests

  • Choose from a variety of contests tailored to your budget and experience. Join thousands of fellow players in a thrilling test of your football knowledge!
  • Compete for attractive rewards and improve your ranking on our leaderboard!

Step 5: Track Your Performance

  • Once the match starts, stay updated on your fantasy team’s performance. Watch live updates and statistics to see how your players are doing in real-time.
  • Earn points through actual game actions like goals, assists, and defensive plays. Every contribution propels you up the leaderboard!

Step 6: Claim Your Prizes!

  • Accumulate points to rise through the leaderboard. Compete for top positions and ultimately claim your prizes!
  • Prepare to celebrate your achievements and enjoy fantastic rewards. Each contest is a new opportunity to win!

Pro Tips for Maximizing Your Wins:

  • Always analyze player statistics and recent performances. Knowledge is your best asset!
  • Consider external factors like weather and pitch conditions—they can significantly affect match outcomes.
  • Engage with our community to exchange strategies and insights. Collaboration enhances your understanding and enjoyment.
  • Stay informed about player injuries and last-minute changes, as they can impact your team’s performance!
