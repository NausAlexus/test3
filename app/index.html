Frequently Asked Questions

  • 1. How do I register for the EliteUnited app?

    • Answer: To register, download the EliteUnited app from your app store, select "Sign Up," and follow the prompts to create your account using your email address or social media login.

  • 2. How can I manage my fantasy team?

    • Answer: Once you've created a team, you can set your lineup, make trades, and add or drop players directly within the app. You can adjust your team before each game week to optimize performance.

  • 3. How does the scoring system work?

    • Answer: The scoring system is based on player performances in real matches. Points are awarded for goals, assists, clean sheets, and more. Detailed scoring rules can be found in the app's settings.

  • 4. Will I receive live updates during matches?

    • Answer: Yes, the EliteUnited app provides live match updates, including goal notifications, player performances, and your team's scoring in real-time.

  • 5. How can I get support if I have issues with the app?

    • Answer: If you encounter any issues, you can contact our support team via the app's help section, or email us at contact@eliteunited.com for assistance.
